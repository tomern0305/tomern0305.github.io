Here are travel tips for spain

Alhambra

Description: A palace and fortress complex that showcases Moorish architecture. A must-visit in Granada.

Park Güell

Description: A famous public park in Barcelona featuring stunning mosaics and sculptures by Antoni Gaudí. A popular spot for panoramic views of the city.

Catedral de Santiago de Compostela

Description: A stunning cathedral in Galicia, believed to be the final resting place of Saint James the Greater. A pilgrim's paradise.
