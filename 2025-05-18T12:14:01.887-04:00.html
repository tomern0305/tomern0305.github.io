Here are travel tips for spain

Alhambra

Description: A palace and fortress complex that showcases Moorish architecture. It is a breathtaking example of Islamic architecture in the heart of Granada.

Park Güell

Description: A famous public park in Barcelona designed by Antoni Gaudí. It features stunning architectural elements, gardens, and city views.

Cathedral of Santiago de Compostela

Description: A beautiful Catholic cathedral that marks the end of the Camino de Santiago pilgrimage. It is a masterpiece of Romanesque architecture.
