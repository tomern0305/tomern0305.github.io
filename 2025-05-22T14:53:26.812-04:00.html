Here are travel tips for israel

Western Wall

Description: The last remnant of the Second Temple, a historic site of great significance. Also known as the Wailing Wall.

Masada

Description: An ancient fortress, a symbol of Israeli history and heritage, overlooking the Dead Sea. A UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Church of the Holy Sepulchre

Description: A historic church in the Old City of Jerusalem, believed to be the site of Jesus Christ's crucifixion, burial and resurrection.
