Here are travel tips for paris

Eiffel Tower

Description: An iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. Built for the 1889 World's Fair.

Louvre Museum

Description: One of the world's largest and most visited museums, with a collection of art and artifacts from ancient to modern times. Home to the Mona Lisa.

Arc de Triomphe

Description: A triumphal arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France, featuring an eternal flame and a panoramic overlook.
