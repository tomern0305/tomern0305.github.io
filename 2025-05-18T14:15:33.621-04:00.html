Here are travel tips for israel

Western Wall

Description: A historic wall in the Old City, a remnant of the Temple. Considered one of the most sacred sites in Judaism.

Masada National Park

Description: A ancient fortress on top of a rock plateau, a symbol of Jewish heroism. A UNESCO World Heritage Site

Church of the Holy Sepulchre

Description: A church in the Old City, believed to be the place of Jesus Christ's crucifixion. One of the most important pilgrimage sites in Christianity.
