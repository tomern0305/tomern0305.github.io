Here are travel tips for israel

Western Wall

Description: A sacred Jewish site, also known as the Wailing Wall. A remnant of the Second Temple of Jerusalem.

Church of the Holy Sepulchre

Description: A church in the Old City, said to be the site of Jesus' crucifixion and resurrection. A significant Christian pilgrimage site.

Masada

Description: An ancient fortress, symbol of Jewish resistance against the Roman Empire. A UNESCO World Heritage Site.
