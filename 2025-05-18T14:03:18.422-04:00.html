Here are travel tips for israel

Western Wall

Description: One of the holiest sites in Judaism, a remnant of the Second Temple. Visitors can write prayers on notes and insert them between the stones.

Church of the Holy Sepulchre

Description: A church in the Old City that is said to be the site of Jesus' crucifixion and burial. The church is shared by six different Christian denominations.

Masada

Description: An ancient fortification situated on top of a rock plateau, a symbol of the Jewish struggle for independence. Offers breathtaking views of the Dead Sea.
